# Sales-Analytics

📌 Overview

This repository showcases a collection of Sales Analytics projects focused on extracting actionable insights from sales data to drive revenue growth, optimize performance, and support strategic business decisions.

The projects demonstrate expertise in data analysis, reporting, forecasting, and visualization using real-world sales datasets.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze sales performance across products, regions, and time periods
Identify revenue trends and growth opportunities
Optimize pricing and sales strategies
Build dashboards for stakeholder reporting
Enable data-driven decision-making
🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Languages: SQL, Python (Pandas, NumPy)
Visualization: Power BI, Tableau, Excel
Databases: MySQL, PostgreSQL
Other: Data Cleaning, Statistical Analysis, Forecasting Techniques
📂 Project Portfolio
1. 📈 Sales Performance Analysis

Description:
Analyzed historical sales data to evaluate key performance metrics such as revenue, profit, and sales growth.

Key Contributions:

Cleaned and transformed raw sales data
Calculated KPIs (Revenue, Profit Margin, Growth Rate)
Built interactive dashboards for performance tracking

Outcome:
Identified top-performing products and regions, enabling better strategic planning.

2. 🌍 Regional Sales Analysis

Description:
Evaluated sales performance across different geographic regions to uncover market trends.

Key Contributions:

Compared regional revenue and profitability
Identified underperforming regions
Visualized geographic sales distribution

Outcome:
Supported regional strategy optimization and resource allocation.

3. 🛒 Product-Level Analysis

Description:
Analyzed product-wise sales performance to determine high and low-performing items.

Key Contributions:

Identified best-selling and low-performing products
Conducted category-level analysis
Evaluated product contribution to overall revenue

Outcome:
Helped optimize product portfolio and inventory decisions.

4. 📅 Time Series Sales Forecasting

Description:
Forecasted future sales using historical data and statistical models.

Key Contributions:

Performed trend and seasonality analysis
Built forecasting models (moving averages, regression)
Evaluated model accuracy

Outcome:
Enabled better demand planning and inventory management.

5. 💰 Customer Sales Analysis

Description:
Analyzed customer purchasing behavior to identify high-value customers.

Key Contributions:

Segmented customers based on purchase patterns
Calculated customer lifetime value (CLV)
Identified repeat vs. new customers

Outcome:
Improved customer targeting and retention strategies.

Business Insight Generation
